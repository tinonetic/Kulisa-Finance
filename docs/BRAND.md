# Brand Guidelines: Khulisa Modern Minimalist

The Khulisa brand represents **Trust, Growth, and Clarity**. Our design language is inspired by Swiss Minimalism but adapted for a modern South African fintech context.

## Visual Identity

### 1. Color Palette
- **Khulisa Indigo** (`#4F46E5`): Used for primary buttons and success metrics. Represents the "bank-strength" security of our platform.
- **Khulisa Growth Emerald** (`#059669`): Used for inflows and credit score increases. Represents the fertility of the South African soil and personal growth.
- **The Modern Surface** (`#F9FAFB`): All background surfaces are clean and off-white to provide maximum contrast for financial figures.

### 2. Typography
We use **Inter Sans**.
- **Headings**: Semi-bold to Bold, tracking-tight.
- **Financial Data**: Always `tabular-nums` and font-weight 900. Money should look "heavy" and important.
- **Micro-copy**: All-caps, tracked-out (e.g., `tracking-widest`), font-weight 900.

### 3. Components
- **Corners**: Large `rounded-3xl` (24px) corners suggest a soft, approachable, yet premium hardware-like feel.
- **Shadows**: Subtle, natural shadows. We avoid "dirty" gray shadows, preferring indigo-tinted elevations.

## Tone of Voice
Our AI, the **Khulisa Bot**, speaks with a tone that is **Empowering, Direct, and Professional**. It doesn't use jargon; it uses plain language that respects the user's hard work and financial goals.
