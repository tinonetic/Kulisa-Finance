# User Guide: Growing with Khulisa

Welcome to Khulisa! This guide will help you navigate the platform and maximize your financial growth.

## 1. Importing Your Data
To get started, you need to provide the app with your transaction history.

### Supported Format
The app currently accepts **CSV (Comma Separated Values)** files with the following structure:
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Amount**: The numeric value (e.g., 250.00)
- **Description**: The name of the merchant or service.
- **Type**: Either `inflow` (money coming in) or `outflow` (money spent).
- **Category**: (e.g., income, food, transport, top-up).

### How to Import
1. Scroll down to the **Transactions** section.
2. Click **Import CSV**.
3. Select your file. The dashboard will update instantly to reflect your new balance and activity.

## 2. Understanding Your Trust Score
Once your data is imported, click **Analyze & Score** in the Trust Score card.

- **Level 1 (Seed)**: Just starting out. Focus on consistency.
- **Level 2 (Sprout)**: Good patterns. Low-risk micro-loans may become available.
- **Level 3 (Tree)**: Strong financial footprint. Eligible for premium investment paths.

## 3. Starting an Investment
The **Khulisa Bot** analyzes your monthly surplus and suggests a small weekly amount to save (e.g., R50 or R100). 
- Use the **Start Saving** button to lock in your goal for the week.
- These micro-investments are designed to grow without impacting your daily necessities.
